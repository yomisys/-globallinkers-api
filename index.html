Who We Are

Global Linkers Limited is a professional recruitment and HR services company headquartered in Nigeria, with a global reach and a people-first philosophy. We exist to bridge the gap between exceptional talent and forward-thinking organizations.

Founded on the belief that the right placement changes lives — not just careers — we operate with unwavering commitment to integrity, excellence, and efficiency. We serve SMEs, large enterprises, multinationals, and government entities across 36+ industries.

Our brand promise is simple: we link the right people to the right opportunities — every time.
